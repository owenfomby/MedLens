About

  • Foundation of Optimality

    Optimality is a futuristic, innovative technology that aims to use augmented reality technology to help save and improve the lives of people all over the world. We were founded in early 2024 and our aim has always been to develop a game-changing wearable device that is accessible to surgeons worldwide. We hope that Optimality can provide the healthcare sector with an efficient way of improving success rates of thousands of surgeries and procedures. Join us on our journey to redefine the standard of healthcare available to all patients. Welcome to the future of medicine, welcome to Optimality.

  • Meet the team

    Optimality was founded by a passionate, diverse group of students all with the common goal of improving the lives of others through healthcare technology. Each team member used their unique skill sets to contribute to the development and improvement of Optimality. Team members used their backgrounds of programming, design, artificial intelligence and science to contribute to the evolution of the technology.

Frequently Asked Questions

MEDILENS is meticulously programmed with an extensive database of surgical techniques and procedures. When a surgeon interacts with it, the system presents a blueprint-like interface, providing step-by-step instructions precisely when needed during surgeries.

Our goal is to make MEDILENS an indispensable tool in hospitals worldwide. By seamlessly integrating it into surgical workflows, we aim to enhance patient outcomes, reduce complications, and elevate the success rates of diverse surgical procedures.

Currently, MEDILENS is in the beta testing phase, however we are just a few steps away from official launch. We’re fine-tuning its capabilities and actively collecting feedback from medical professionals. During this phase, we’ve also initiated a donation drive to support further development.
